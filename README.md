## Featured Project: Automated Retail Pricing & Rebate Engine
**Assignment:** Term Project

### Business Case
Developed an end-to-end automated billing system for a retail bakery to optimize product packaging and automate tiered pricing models. The system addresses the logistical challenge of breaking down bulk orders into the most cost-effective packet sizes while applying volume-based incentives.

### Technical Implementation
* **Systems Analysis:** Conducted a full SDLC Phase 1 analysis, mapping packet distribution and rebate eligibility through logical flowcharts.
* **Packaging Logic:** Engineered a solution using Integer Division and Modulus Operators to calculate the optimal breakdown of 100-count, 25-count, and single-unit packets.
* **Pricing Engine:** Programmed a dynamic cost calculator with three distinct price points ($1.10, $1.25, and $1.50) and a conditional $2.00 rebate for high-volume orders (>400 units).
* **Precision:** Employed `printf` formatting for financial reporting accuracy.

## Tiered Payroll & Regulatory Compliance Engine
**Assignment:** Lab #4

### Business Case
Engineered a payroll system to model labor contract standards, ensuring compliance with "Minimum Pay" guarantees and dynamic tax brackets.

### Key Features
* **Labor Standard Automation:** Programmed a "Minimum Pay" rule ensuring all employees are compensated for at least 40 hours of labor, reflecting standard union contract requirements.
* **Dynamic Tax Logic:** Developed a tiered tax engine that shifts between 1.5% and 1.61% based on a $1,000 gross salary threshold.
* **Audit Readiness:** Standardized outputs to two decimal places for professional financial reporting.
